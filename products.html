Teja S17 Dried Red Chilli

Teja chilli, one of the hottest varieties available, is primarily grown in southern India, near the renowned Guntur Mirchi Market. Known globally as S17 Teja chilli, it is highly sought after by international buyers. Our Teja chillies are offered in stemless, with stem, and powdered forms, making them perfect for soups, stir-fries, stews, and spice blends. Ideal for long-term storage, Teja chilli combines intense heat with exceptional quality.

  • Color: Deep red with a glossy finish
  • Skin: Thin yet firm, with fewer seeds
  • Aroma: Pungent, earthy fragrance
  • Shape: Pointed tip with medium-length body
  • Pungency: High, ideal for spicing up dishes
  • Processing: Sun-dried
  • Capsaicin Percentage: 0.589%
  • Length: 5-7 cm (2-3 inches)
  • Heat Level: 50,000 to 100,000 SHU
  • ASTA Color Value: 75-100
  • Moisture: 8-10%

334 S4/Sannam Dried Red Chilli

Guntur Sannam chilli, also known as S4, is renowned for its bright red color and moderate heat, perfect for enhancing both flavor and appearance in dishes. Grown in the rich soils of Andhra Pradesh's Guntur, Khammam, and Prakasam districts, this chilli is globally recognized for its consistent quality. With its thick skin, Guntur Sannam delivers bold flavor whether used as a powder or crushed, making it a top choice for spice blends, curries, and sauces.

  • Color: Deep red, glossy texture

  • Aroma: Mild, fruity & earthy undertone

  • Pungency: Moderate heat
  • Processing: Traditionally sun-dried
  • Capsaicin Percentage: 0.226
  • Length: 5-8 cm (2-3 inches)
  • Heat Level: 20,000 to 35,000 SH
  • ASTA Color Value: 60-80
  • Moisture: 10-12%

Turmeric

Turmeric is a vibrant yellow-orange spice known for its earthy flavor and numerous health benefits. Grown primarily in India, turmeric is a key ingredient in many culinary dishes and traditional medicines. It is valued for its anti-inflammatory properties and is often used in powdered form for cooking, as well as in supplements and skincare products.

  • Color: Bright yellow to deep orange
  • Form: Whole dried rhizome or ground powder
  • Aroma: Earthy, slightly bitter, and peppery
  • Flavor: Warm, bitter, and slightly pungent
  • Curcumin Content: 2-5%
  • Processing: Boiled, dried, and ground
  • Moisture: 6-12%
  • Length: 3-7 cm (rhizome)
  • Uses: Culinary, medicinal, cosmetic, dye
  • Origin: India (major producer)
  • Health Benefits: Anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, supports digestion

Coriander Seeds

Coriander seeds are the dried seeds of the coriander plant, known for their warm, nutty, and citrusy flavor. Widely used in cooking and traditional medicine, they are valued for their digestive and anti-inflammatory properties. Coriander seeds are commonly ground into a spice or used whole in various cuisines around the world.

  • Color: Light brown to yellowish-brown
  • Form: Whole dried seeds or ground powder
  • Aroma: Warm, spicy, and citrusy
  • Flavor: Mild, nutty, slightly sweet with citrus notes
  • Essential Oil Content: 0.1-1%
  • Processing: Sun-dried and cleaned
  • Moisture: 8-12%
  • Seed Size: 3-5 mm diameter
  • Uses: Culinary (whole or ground), pickling, spice blends
  • Origin: India, Morocco, Russia, and others
  • Health Benefits: Aids digestion, anti-inflammatory, rich in antioxidants

Chilli Flakes

Chilli flakes are crushed dried red chillies, known for their spicy heat and vibrant color. Commonly used as a condiment or spice in various cuisines, they add a fiery kick to dishes. Chilli flakes are valued for their capsaicin content, which provides heat and potential health benefits such as metabolism boosting and pain relief.

  • Color: Bright to deep red with visible seeds
  • Form: Crushed dried red chillies (flakes)
  • Aroma: Pungent, spicy, and slightly smoky
  • Flavor: Hot, sharp, and robust
  • Heat Level: Varies by variety, typically 15,000–50,000 SHU
  • Processing: Sun-dried, then crushed
  • Moisture: 8-12%
  • Seed Content: Contains both skin and seeds
  • Uses: Pizza, pasta, curries, marinades, pickles, sauces
  • Origin: India (major producer), also China, Turkey
  • Health Benefits: Rich in capsaicin, boosts metabolism, antioxidant
